Complete crew visibility

Monitor the positions of all on-duty personnel from the bridge in real-time. Quickly track and trace the locations of any crew member during an emergency to find their last known position.

Monitor third-party contractor activity whilst the vessel is docked or during a voyage. Historical locations can also be accessed in the case of a security breach.

Low-cost installation

Hyperfix does not require large amounts of new infrastructure and can be integrated with existing ship plans and blueprints to provide compartment-level crew positions.

A wireless mesh-network is maintained by Hyperfix to provide coverage throughout the vessel without the need for expensive cabling.

Real-time location information

Identify the location of any crew member in seconds and see a log of their previous positions. Hyperfix can provide timelines deck by deck to give unparalleled visibility into crew activity.

Private zones can also be configured so that off-duty personnel will only be tracked once outside of them.

The Company

Hyperfix is a start-up born from shared interest and targeted towards creating a safer environment for mariners. The company was founded in Autumn of 2019 with a vision to create the most reliable local positioning systems for rugged environments. Our propitiatory systems are built with knowledge transferred from multiple different sectors, an approach that we believe is an industry first for marine safety technology. By combining in-depth domain expertise in commercial shipping, cyber security and machine learning, Hyperfix has the tools at hand to deliver the most robust tracking systems available on the market today.
